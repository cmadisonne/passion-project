What is The Pond


The Pond is an open and growing community for yogis to begin and continue their yoga journey.
From beginner to advanced, we offer just the right sessions, personalized to your needs and wants.
Here at the pond, we encourage physical, mental, and spiritual health. You can schedule a
yoga session, find upcoming events in the city, or just enjoy the content. Feel free to wander around The Pond.
Namaste



About Me




Peace, I am Lotus. I have been on my spiritual journey for three years. I started doing yoga
when I was 17 years old due to my struggle with anxiety and depression. Yoga instantly led me to
a more peaceful path in life. About 2 years after I began my journey, I gained a greater purpose
to share the knowledge and peace with those around me. I believe spiritual and mental health should
be a priority for everyone, especially in my community. So, I began spreading my peaceful and loving
energy through my yoga classes. I am humble to say that my journey has come a long way, and I hope
that you will join me in the continued growth of The Pond.
